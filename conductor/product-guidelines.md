# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The tone of JournalFX's user-facing content will strike a balance between professional authority and encouraging support. We aim to provide clear, direct, and actionable information, fostering user confidence and promoting a sense of continuous growth in their trading journey.

## Brand Messaging
Our core message is about user empowerment. JournalFX is designed to equip traders with the necessary tools, insights, and structure to confidently take control of their trading journey, cultivate discipline, and achieve their financial objectives.

## Visual Identity
JournalFX will feature a modern and sleek visual design, characterized by clean lines and a minimalist aesthetic that prioritizes clarity and readability. The overall look and feel will be trustworthy and professional, building confidence, while dynamic data visualizations will effectively represent market activity and trading performance metrics.
